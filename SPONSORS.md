## Become a Sponsor

CIV.IQ is free, open source, and independent. Your sponsorship helps cover:

- Server and hosting costs
- Government API fees (Congress.gov, FEC, OpenStates, Census)
- Development and maintenance time
- New feature development

**Sponsor on GitHub:** [github.com/sponsors/civdotiq](https://github.com/sponsors/civdotiq)
