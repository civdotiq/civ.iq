## Core Philosophical Concepts

### The CivicRank Algorithm

Just as PageRank measured importance through connections, CivicRank reveals political influence through:

- Legislative success (bills passed, amendments adopted)
- Network centrality (committee positions, coalition leadership)
- Financial influence (funding received and distributed)
- Public impact (constituent engagement, media presence)

### Natural Discovery Paths

Instead of forcing users to understand government structure, they can:

- Start with personal concerns ("Why is my prescription expensive?")
- Follow natural connections (bills → sponsors → donors → influence)
- Discover power structures through exploration, not explanation

### The Web of Power

Government isn't a hierarchy; it's a web of connections:

```
Traditional: Congress → Committees → Members
Our Vision: Issue → Bills → Representatives → Donors → Influence → Action
```
